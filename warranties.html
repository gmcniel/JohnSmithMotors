Warranty Information

Our vehicles come with a limited warranty that covers certain repairs and services for a specified period of time. Please refer to the warranty documentation provided with your vehicle for detailed information.
Used vehicles come with a limited warranty that covers certain repairs and services for a specified period of time. Please refer to the warranty documentation provided with your vehicle for detailed information.
Used vehicles with a limited warranty to where any mechanical malfunction occurs within the warranty period will be covered for repairs free of charge, especially free battery replacement if the battery provided is defective. Any aftermarket modifications may void the warranty.
All used vehicles sold come with a 10,000 mile or 12 month warranty, whichever comes first. This warranty covers the engine, transmission, and drivetrain components. Please contact our service department for more information on warranty coverage and terms.
